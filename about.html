About PMELL

More Than Just Baseball

Pocono Mountain East Little League gives local kids a place to learn the game, build confidence, make friends, and grow through teamwork, sportsmanship, and community.

Register Today →

Our Mission

Building Players. Building Character.

PMELL is a volunteer-powered youth baseball and softball organization focused on creating a positive, welcoming environment for players and families.

From first swings to competitive game situations, our goal is to help every player improve, compete, and enjoy being part of something bigger than themselves.

✓
Teach the gameFundamentals, confidence, teamwork, and fair play.
★
Support familiesClear communication, helpful resources, and a welcoming league culture.
❤
Strengthen communityLocal volunteers, sponsors, coaches, and families working together.

League Programs

Divisions For Every Stage

From beginners learning the basics to older players developing game awareness and competition, PMELL offers a path for players to grow.

Tee Ball

An introduction to baseball and softball with a focus on fun, confidence, and basic skills.

Coach Pitch

A bridge between beginner play and player development, with coaches helping players learn the flow of the game.

Minors

Players continue developing fundamentals, position awareness, hitting, throwing, and teamwork.

Majors

A more competitive division where players sharpen skills and prepare for higher levels of play.

Softball

Opportunities for girls to learn, compete, and grow through PMELL softball programs.

Juniors

Older players continue their development with a larger-field experience and more advanced competition.
