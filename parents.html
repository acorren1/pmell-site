New To PMELL?

What Families Should Know

A quick guide for families joining PMELL for the first time.

01

First Time Registering?

Registration is handled through Sports Connect. Once registration opens, families can choose available programs based on eligibility and league age.

02

What Equipment Do I Need?

Most players need a glove, cleats, baseball or softball pants, and water bottle. Division-specific equipment needs can vary by age and level.

03

What Happens After Registration?

After registration, families receive updates about evaluations, team placement, practices, schedules, and league communication.

Parent FAQ

Common Questions

How do I know what division my child is in?

Start with the PMELL division guide and Sports Connect registration. Division placement may depend on age, eligibility, experience, and tryouts.

Where are schedules posted?

Schedules can be linked here once finalized. PMELL can also use this page for quick updates and announcements.

Can I volunteer if I am not coaching?

Yes. Concessions, field prep, events, fundraising, and league support are all important ways to help.

How do concession signups work?

PMELL can list available shifts online and collect signups through the existing concessions system.
