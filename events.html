Common Questions

Where are schedules posted?

PMELL can link official schedules here and use the Events page for league-wide dates and announcements.

How are rainouts communicated?

Rainout updates can be posted on this page, shared through league communication channels, and added to the calendar when needed.

Can events change?

Yes. Weather, field conditions, and league needs can affect event timing. Always check the latest updates.

Can fundraisers be listed here?

Yes. Restaurant nights, community events, tournaments, and fundraisers are great fits for this page.
